Title: Heads or Tails 86
Author: Steve Russell (srussell@netunlimited.net)
Version 1.0
Platform: TI-86
Released: 8-17-97

Description: HOT is a simple betting game.  You start with $100 and bet on a coin flip.
Gain or lose money, and keep on betting.  The object of the game is to amass $5,000.  Easy
to learn, but only a truly lucky (or extremely bored) person can win.
