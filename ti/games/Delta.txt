Delta_Attack

The object of this game is to destroy all the delta signs that appear on the 
screen.  You are the icon on the left.  Use the up and down arrow keys to 
move, and the 2nd key to fire.  Fire when LOCK appears.  You have a limited 
amount of time to destroy each delta sign before it destroys you.  Points 
gained on each level are porportional to the time remaining.
