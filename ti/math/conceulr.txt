conceulr
--------

for TI-86

This program finds the Centroid, Circumcenter, and orthocenter of a triangle
given the
coordinates of the 3 vertices.  Then it will find the equation of the Euler
line using these
points.  The results will be displayed in 2 forms, as decimals and fractions.

requires 1128 bytes of calculator memory.
By MAtt Andel
